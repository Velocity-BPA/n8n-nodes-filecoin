# Automate Filecoin and IPFS with n8n: Introducing the n8n-nodes-filecoin Community Node

We're excited to announce the release of n8n-nodes-filecoin, a powerful new community node that brings decentralized storage capabilities directly into your n8n workflows. Created by Velocity BPA, this node bridges the gap between no-code automation and Web3 storage infrastructure.

## The Problem: Web3 Storage Meets Automation

As organizations increasingly explore decentralized technologies, integrating blockchain-based storage solutions into existing workflows has remained technically challenging. Developers and automation specialists need a way to interact with Filecoin's decentralized storage network, deploy FEVM smart contracts, and leverage IPFS without writing complex code or managing multiple APIs.

## What n8n-nodes-filecoin Brings to Your Workflows

This community node unlocks three powerful capabilities within your n8n automation workflows:

**Decentralized Storage Operations**: Store and retrieve data on the Filecoin network, ensuring your files benefit from blockchain-verified, distributed storage. Perfect for archival, backup automation, and compliance workflows that require immutable storage proof.

**FEVM Smart Contract Integration**: Interact with Filecoin's Ethereum Virtual Machine (FEVM) to deploy and execute smart contracts. Automate contract deployments, trigger contract functions, and integrate Web3 logic into your business processes.

**IPFS Integration**: Seamlessly add and retrieve content from IPFS, enabling content-addressed storage in your workflows. Ideal for NFT metadata management, distributed content delivery, and decentralized application backends.

## Real-World Use Cases

Imagine automatically archiving important business documents to Filecoin when they're uploaded to your cloud storage, creating an immutable audit trail. Or triggering smart contract executions based on events from traditional APIs. The possibilities span from Web3 startups to enterprises exploring blockchain technology.

## Getting Started

Installation is straightforward. In your n8n instance, run:


npm install n8n-nodes-filecoin


After restarting n8n, you'll find the Filecoin node available in your node palette, ready to drag into your workflows.
